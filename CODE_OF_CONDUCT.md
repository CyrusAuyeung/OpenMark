# Code of Conduct

OpenMark should be a calm and useful place to build a focused Markdown editor.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that helps the project:

- Use welcoming and direct language.
- Respect different writing workflows and operating systems.
- Keep criticism focused on the code, design, or product tradeoff.
- Assume contributors are here in good faith.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers have asked to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code of conduct. Contributors who repeatedly violate it may be blocked from participating in the project.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub where possible. Keep reports concise and include links, screenshots, or timestamps when relevant.
